About The Group

Stray Kids is an 8-member K-pop boy group under JYP Entertainment that debuted on March 25, 2018. They're known for producing their own music, with powerful lyrics and intense performances. The members are Bang Chan (leader and producer from Australia), Lee Know (main dancer), Changbin (rapper with a deep voice), Hyunjin (visual and dancer), Han (rapper and vocalist), Felix (deep-voiced Aussie sweetheart), Seungmin (main vocalist), and I.N (the youngest, known for his bright smile). Their fans are called STAY, and Stray Kids' music often talks about youth, identity, and being true to yourself, all with a strong and unique sound.
